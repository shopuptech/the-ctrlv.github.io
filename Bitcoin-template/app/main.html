  • How is Bitcoin stored?

    Any crypto is stored in a digital wallet. Imagine your Bitcoin like a computer file stored in a Bitcoin wallet. A Bitcoin wallet can be hot, online-based, or cold, hardware-based. Hot wallets are very convenient because you can access your Bitcoin wallet with ease. Similarly to an online bank account or PayPal, you can access your wallet through any device connected to the Internet. Almost any cryptocurrency exchange offers hot wallets. If you decide to open a hot wallet, privacy issues may arise. Your hot wallet may be compromised if the exchange service gets hacked. Cold wallets, on the other hand, provide safer options to store your private key, either on paper or a USB device. Research different digital wallet providers before you choose a cold wallet and decide on how to store your private key.
