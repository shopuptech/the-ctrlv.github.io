What is included with Direct Cremation?

Direct Cremation includes Basic Services of the Funeral Director and Staff, Transfer to or from the crematory, Transfer of the Remains to the Funeral Establishment, securing the necessary authorization, and crematory fee. The funeral homes will offer a heavy cardboard container from their selection.

Turnaround

Standard turnaround time is between 3 to 14 days. Because of the demand and high volume of arrangements we will provide you with updates regarding when preparations are concluded to make your arrangements and we will set up a day to pick up the ramains.
