Upper third of the
face

Removal of frown lines

Aesthetic treatment of frown lines (glabella): botulinum and hyaluron filler relax frown lines and smooth them for a harmonious facial expression.

From CHF 350 + More about the treatment of frown lines

Removal of forehead wrinkles

Aesthetic treatment of forehead wrinkles (worry lines): botulinum (botulinum toxin) in combination with hyaluron makes wrinkles and other creases on the forehead and face disappear.

From CHF 350 + More about the forehead wrinkle removal

Hollow forehead contour

The forehead area may also experience a loss of volume of the skin and subcutaneous tissue, causing a lowering of the eyebrows, prominence of the eye bone ridges or an irregular contour of the forehead. Instead of a tapered forehead contour, it becomes concave in certain areas, causing a visual disturbance and aesthetically unpleasing. The treatment with hyaluron compensates for the loss of volume.

From CHF 350 + More about the hollow forehead contour

Hollow temple contour

The volume loss is also partially distinct at this point, which leads to a bony and therefore unhealthy appearance. The class="chf"sinking of the temple leads to a shortening of the eyebrow sides when looking from the front. It can also lead to a sagging of the eyebrows laterally. Strongly cross-linked hyaluronic acid can correct all these problems. This region is a challenging one, which should only be treated by a specialist.
