AESTHETIC MEDICINE
IN ZURICH

Dr. Dan Iselin

Dr. Iselin is an aesthetic physician in Zurich who specialises in non-surgical facial treatments. For the last ten years he has dedicated himself entirely to the gentle and natural application of botulinum and hyaluron. While these methods are only two of the many options on the market, they represent the foundation of every modern aesthetic medicine treatment.

Dr. Iselin’s approach to treatment places great emphasis on the effectiveness and safety of the chosen method, the natural look of the result and respect for the individual. Holistic integrity is the core and focus of his Zurich clinic.

Dr. Iselin’s beauty clinic is located in the heart of Zurich and is easily accessible by car or public transport.
